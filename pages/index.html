What can you build?

Frontends for Any Backend

Webfoundry is a frontend development tool that gives you the full untethered power of raw JavaScript. Import your favorite npm modules from unpkg/esm.sh (e.g. Supabase Client) or run native fetch against your own backend servers.

Fully Serverless P2P Apps

Use the amazing Trystero library to directly connect your users via WebRTC.

LLM Clients

Most LLM service providers like OpenAI and xAI are CORS-enabled, meaning you can build BYOK apps that connect directly to their servers.

Internal Use Tools

With its ease of use and unlimited potential, Webfoundry stands as a reasonable alternative to no-code/low-code tools such as Retool.

Desktop and Mobile Apps

Package your Webfoundry project as a native desktop or mobile app using built-in Tauri exports.

The sky is the limit!

Since Webfoundry is not built on a vDOM-based reactivity library, it plays well with vanilla JS libs, jQuery, and more.
